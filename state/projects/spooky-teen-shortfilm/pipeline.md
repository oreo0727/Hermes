# Production Pipeline (AI-Generated Short)

Goal: Reproducible path from script to final in ~1 week of focused work.

Phases
1) Script & Boards (Day 12)
- Lock logline + beat sheet.
- Generate styleframes (1015 key images) to freeze look.
- Cast VO (teen-friendly tone). Record scratch reads.

2) Animatic (Day 23)
- Assemble boards + temp VO + temp SFX.
- Validate flow, timing, and scare beats.

3) Final Visuals (Day 35)
- Shot list to renders: 2030 shots @ 410 sec.
- Maintain aspect-safe framing (16:9 center safe for 9:16).
- Light grade + consistent grain.

4) Sound & Mix (Day 56)
- Original cues: drone, motif, sting, button.
- Foley: hallway, gym squeak, PA hiss.
- Mix for earbuds first; LUFS 14 target.

5) Finish & Deliver (Day 67)
- Captions, credits, AI disclosure card.
- Masters: 4K 16:9; 1080x1920 vertical cut; 1080 square; clean + burned subs versions.

Asset Checklist
- Script v1.0 (locked), Beat Sheet, Shot List, Style Bible, Board Pack, VO Script, Cue Sheet, SFX List, Final Renders, Mix Stems, Captions (.srt), Credits.

Acceptance
- Runtime 57min; 23 memorable images; 1 signature sound; age-appropriate; export set complete.
