# Visual Reference Board (Inspiration)

Source: user-provided campfire scene (5 teens around fire, lantern, tent, sign “Camp Pinewood”, moon/stars).

Production Cues We Mirror
- Lighting: warm fire key, lantern practical left FG, cool moon rim; low ambient. Target contrasts: key:fill ≈ 6:1; moon rim ≈ 0.15 key.
- Composition: central fire; semicircle seating on logs; tent back-center; sign on right trunk; lantern left FG.
- Palette: forest greens/browns + cool night blues; fire oranges/yellows; teal/green lantern; olive tent.
- Wardrobe silhouettes: hoodies/caps/utility pants/boots (we keep variety via accents; optional camo overlay).
- Prop set: rock ring, roasting sticks/marshmallow, backpacks, bedroll, lantern, tent, sign.

Differences (our film)
- Tone shifts from cozy to uncanny: loop rule, missing chair, phones-only darkness, Live Photo silhouette.
- Battery-persistence visual beat and time-pop refractive effect.
