# Styleframe Shortlist (10) — Campfire Repeat (3D)

Guiding look: Stylized 3D (NPR). Moonlit blues vs. campfire ambers; phone screens as fill. Sub–Rated R intensity.

1) Embers Establishing
- Composition: Wide lockoff of the camp ring, dead coals pulsing. Fog rows in treeline. One phone face-down glows edges.
- Palette: Deep teal woods, ember orange.

2) First Relight — Time Pop
- CU on match + kindling; whoosh. Subtle refractive ripple and lens-breath push as time snaps.
- Rim light outlines faces; ash motes bloom.

3) Battery Persists
- OTS on Avas phone: lockscreen 12:00 AM; battery still 3%. Background bokeh of the same fire from prior memory.

4) Marker Persists
- Stylized stack of three river stones near a marked birch; the stones remain identical across loops; leaf positions shift.

5) Loop Montage Beat
- Graphic match cut: relightdouserelight; chairs shuffle; mug migrates; expressions sour.

6) Missing Chair
- Camp wide: one empty chair reads as wrong. Shallow DOF isolates it; headlamps paint silhouettes.

7) Live Photo Silhouette
- Phone OTS playing Live Photo scrub: a figure registers in treeline only during motion.

8) Darkness Choice
- All light off except faint moon; phones under-chin; coals buried; breathing visible; insects cut to silence.

9) Near-Ignition Flicker
- Macro CU on a coal that almost catches. Fingers hover, resisting. Tiny flare dies.

10) Dawn Button
- Cool-to-warm gradient through trees; long lens. A hand pockets a faintly glowing coal. Out-of-focus lighter click behind.
