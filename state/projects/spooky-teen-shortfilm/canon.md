# Midnight Signals — AI Spooky Short for Teens Canon

This canon records durable rules and boundaries for the project.

## Non-Negotiables (Locked)
- Rating boundary: Sub–Rated R. Target PG-13 intensity; we may edge to TV-14 feel but explicitly avoid R content.
- Format: Animated short (5–7 minutes). Stylized 3D (primary) with option for 2D accents; cinematic framing with vertical-safe center.
- Safety: No real-world doxxing, self-harm depiction, or exploitative humiliation. No slurs. No sexualized content.
- Authorship: Clear AI-use disclosure in end card/description.

## Content Guidelines
Allowed (within PG-13):
- Suspense, menace, jump scares, implied peril; limited non-graphic blood (brief), brief intense sequences.
- Thematic fear around technology, isolation, and friendship stakes.
- Supernatural/uncanny elements without explicit occult ritual detail.

Prohibited (pushes into R or not appropriate):
- Graphic gore, lingering injury detail, realistic brutality, torture.
- Sexual content, nudity, exploitation, or harassment-as-scare.
- Hate speech, slurs, real-person targeting, or doxxing-like specifics.
- Prolonged cruelty or humiliation of characters as punchline.

## Product And World Rules
- Diegesis: Uncanny manifests through believable channels: phones, radios, signage, and environmental systems.
- Agency: The friend group makes choices; consequences are story-driven, not arbitrary punishment.
- Tech Aesthetic: Modern smartphones + light analog grit; overlays are legible and purposeful.

## Voice And Visual Direction
- Tone: Tense, empathetic, curious. Humor bleeds tension without undercutting scares.
- Color: Night woods: cool moonlit blues; campfire ambers/oranges; controlled red for alerts.
- Camera: Grounded/handheld feel; occasional security-cam or drone-like lockoffs.
- Animation: Stylized 3D (toon/NPR with rim lights, volumetric fog, ember particles); 12–24 fps elasticity; light VHS/CRT bloom sparingly.

## Setting Tracks
- School Track (original): Midnight Signals — Detention kids discover the school PA reads their unsent messages aloud—and then messages from five minutes in the future.
- Woods Track (current focus): Five teens on an overnight camp; cell service dies; uncanny signal behavior emerges from the forest. See concepts_forest_3d.md for variants.
