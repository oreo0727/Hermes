# Beat Sheet: Midnight Signals (5–7 min)

Premise: Detention kids discover the school PA reads their group chat aloud—but only the messages they never sent. It escalates from creepy to personal, forcing a choice: cut the signal or let it finish the sentence.

Cast: 4 friends (Ava—skeptic, Jay—techy, Noor—empathetic, Eli—class clown). Antagonist: The PA system (uncanny voice; not a person).

Structure (10 beats)
1) Cold Open (0:00–0:30) — Empty hallway, flicker. PA crackles: “You are not alone.” Title sting.
2) Setup (0:30–1:00) — Detention room. Teacher steps out. Group chat pops: memes; inside jokes. PA reads a private draft Ava wrote but never sent.
3) Rule Discovery (1:00–1:45) — They test it. Draft a message; don’t send. After 8 sec, PA reads it verbatim. The voice is off—wrong cadence, like it learned their tone.
4) First Turn (1:45–2:15) — A cruel draft from months ago (Jay) is read aloud. Tension spikes among friends.
5) Investigation (2:15–3:00) — Jay traces cables to the PA booth. Camera POV shifts to security cams. On-screen meters pulse to their typing.
6) Midpoint (3:00–3:45) — The PA starts reading messages from five minutes in the future. A line reveals Noor alone in the gym—she’s in the room now.
7) Split (3:45–4:30) — Eli and Ava go to the gym to “break the loop.” Jay stays to pull the breaker. Cross-cut: phone screens, motion cams, footsteps.
8) Confrontation (4:30–5:30) — In the gym, the PA reads a confession Noor never typed. It’s kind, brave, and too specific. “If you cut the line, it forgets us.”
9) Choice (5:30–6:15) — Jay at the panel hesitates. Ava orders: “Cut it.” Jay pulls the breaker. Silence… then battery backup whines. The PA whispers: “You sent it.”
10) Button (6:15–7:00) — Morning bell. Normal lighting. On their phones: a queued message sent at 12:01 AM from none of their devices: just a location pin labeled “PA Room.” Final image: a red LED still blinking in the booth.

Visuals & Sound
- Visual: clean school liminal spaces, cool greens/ambers, phone overlays diegetic.
- Sound: hiss, shortwave scans, reversed pads, PA formants, heartbeat sidechain.

Safety & Boundaries
- No gore, no slurs, no humiliation. Fear is uncanny agency + friendship strain.
