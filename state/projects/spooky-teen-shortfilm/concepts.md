# Concept Pack (Teen Spooky Short)

Six high-concept loglines
1) Midnight Signals: Detention kids discover the school PA reads their group chat aloudbut only the messages they never sent.
2) Dead Zone: A friend group loses cell signal along one hallway. Their phones still stream live video from five minutes in the future.
3) Lost & Found: Borrowed hoodies show up in the schools lost-and-found each night, tagged with notes written in their owners handwritingfrom last year.
4) Bus 404: The late bus tracker app adds a route that doesnt exist. If you follow it, it shows you where youll be strandedaloneafter midnight.
5) Mirror Club: A campus mirror selfie challenge starts trending. Reflections lag by one framethen one secondthen start making decisions first.
6) Detour: The navigation app inside the school (for new students) suggests a detour during a lockdown drillinto a corridor nobody remembers.

Style packages (pick one or hybrid)
A) Liminal NeoAnalog: Clean digital (phones, screens) + tastefully sparse VHS overlays; cool greens, sodium vapor ambers, and CRT bloom; bold typography for timestamps.
B) AfterSchool Horror: Warm hallways, sunset orange to deep blue night; handheld phone POV; subtitles as diegetic overlay (screen recordings, notification toasts).
C) Sensor Watch: Found footage from motion cams (gym, cafeteria, bus stop); security UI motifs; blooms, posterized shadows; glitch audio motifs.

Tone & Scare Design
- Suspense > shock. 21 build; 12 payoffs. Never humiliate the characters.
- PG1: Implied danger and uncanny behavior; no gore.
- Audio carries fear: intercom hiss, reversed choral pad, distant gym squeak, HVAC rumble, modulated PA voice.

Anchors we can reuse
- Phone lockscreen + chat overlays
- School map with dead corridor
- Intercom/PA booth with VU meters
- Bus stop at blue hour with LED arrival board
